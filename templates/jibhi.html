Jibhi Waterfalls

One of the most favorite and pristine places to spend your time while in Jibhi is the waterfall. It is an absolutely quiet and peaceful walk to the waterfall. And about a kilometer, before you reach, you start hearing the sound of water falling on a rock and that is the most enchanting and peaceful sound in the whole universe. This is a hidden gem in the Himalayas

Serolsar Lake

Serolsar lake is located at a distance of 6 km from Jalori Pass. This lake can be reached by a trek from Jibhi or Jalori Pass. Serolsar Lake is hidden amidst dense forests covered with pine trees.The water in the river is very clear and the perfect shade of blue. The water of the lake is also believed to have medicinal properties.

Mini Thailand

Situated amidst the rich forest area, Mini Thailand is a hidden Gem in Jibhi. The place gets its name from the hut-type structure of stone along with a pond on the path of the river. Tourists called it Mini Thailand while the locals called this place Kuli Katandi. The water of the Pond is so crystal & clear that even you can swim inside it.
