Kodaikanal Lake

You take an above-ground view of the Kodaikanal Lake, and you will be amazed to find a star-shaped water body that is the soul of this city. What makes this exceptional amongst the other places to visit in Kodaikanal is the fact that it is man-made and dates many years back. The Palani Hills ranges make an extraordinary surrounding the main lake

Canopy Hill

One of the best places to visit in Kodaikanal offers you a grand show of nature with the background music of the birds. The mountain peaks are covered with green grass, moving clouds and fresh air. You get an excuse to do a 15 KM trek in the dense Western Ghat forest while easily spotting some wildlife creatures like Bison, wild goats, porcupines, etc.

Bear Sholla Falls

If you are looking for privacy, serenity, a glimpse at nature’s beauty and want to say hello to some exotic species and fauna, then a visit to Bear Shola Falls is a must! It gets this attention-grabbing name from the fact that Bears visited this water body to quench their thirst
